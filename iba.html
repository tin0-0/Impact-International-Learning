IBA — International Behavior Analyst Course

An advanced professional certification pathway preparing candidates to design, implement, supervise, and evaluate behaviour-analytic services across educational, clinical, and community settings.

Course Overview

What Is the IBA Course?

The International Behavior Analyst (IBA) course is a comprehensive advanced certification programme built for professionals who want to lead and deliver behaviour-analytic services at the highest level.

Through six comprehensive modules, participants develop deep expertise across the full scope of Applied Behaviour Analysis — from foundational principles to advanced treatment planning — equipping them for leadership and supervisory roles in educational, clinical, and community settings.

Programme Content

6 Core Modules

The IBA course is structured around six comprehensive modules, each building on the last to develop complete clinical and professional competency.

Module 01

Principles & Characteristics of Behaviour

Module 02

Autism Spectrum Disorders & ABA Interventions

Module 03

Behaviour Assessment & Functional Analysis

Module 04

Ethics, Personnel Management & Supervision

Module 05

Experimental Evaluation of Interventions

Module 06

Behaviour Change Procedures & Treatment Planning

Certification Pathway

Your Path to IBA Certification

Upon successful completion of all six modules, participants will meet the educational requirements for IBA certification through the International Behavior Analysis Organization (IBAO) and the Qualified Applied Behavior Analysis Credentialing Board (QABA). Our training programmes are board-approved and designed to fulfil the educational components of the certification pathway.

Supervision support is available through Impact International Learning to help candidates complete their required fieldwork and supervision hours as part of the full certification process.

Course Outcome

Eligibility to pursue IBA certification requirements and advanced practice opportunities in Applied Behaviour Analysis — equipping you to lead, supervise, and deliver behaviour-analytic services at the highest professional standard.
