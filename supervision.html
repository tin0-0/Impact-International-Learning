Supervision Services

Structured, ethical, and competency-based supervision supporting candidates pursuing professional certification in Applied Behaviour Analysis — bridging the gap between theory and real-world practice.

Service Overview

What Are Our Supervision Services?

Our Supervision Services support candidates pursuing professional certification in Applied Behaviour Analysis through structured, ethical, and competency-based supervision. Supervision is a required component of the IBA, IBT, and ABAT certification pathways — and Regai Peter Zata provides professional, board-aligned supervision to help candidates complete their fieldwork requirements and develop real confidence as behaviour practitioners.

Supervision is designed to help candidates bridge the gap between theory and practice while meeting certification requirements and developing the professional confidence to deliver effective, ethical behaviour-analytic services.

Certification Pathway

How Supervision Fits Your Certification Journey

1

Complete Your Training Course

Enrol in and complete the IBA, IBT, or ABAT course with Impact International Learning to meet the educational requirements for certification.

2

Begin Supervised Fieldwork

Start your required fieldwork hours under Regai's professional supervision — structured sessions that count toward your certification requirements.

3

Develop Competency & Confidence

Regular case consultations, performance feedback, and mentoring to ensure you're meeting competency standards and developing real-world skills.

4

Achieve Certification

Complete your supervised hours, pass your exam, and achieve your IBA, IBT, or ABAT certification with the support you need every step of the way.

Service Outcome

Development of practical competencies, professional confidence, and successful progression toward IBA, IBT, or ABAT certification — supported by structured, ethical supervision from a UAE Ministry of Education licensed SEN Teacher and Behaviour Analyst.
